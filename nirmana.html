Nirmana

  1. Home
  2. Nirmana

Nirmana is a basic concept of art and design to understanding deep visual elements such as shape, color, texture and composition. Nirmana has two types such as Nirmana 2D (Dwimatra) and Nirmana 3D (Trimatra). For visual communication design students, nirmana is important to learn because nirmana can help exercise creativity, create innovation in visual elements and improve the taste of design.
